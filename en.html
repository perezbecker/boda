GETTING THERE

Fly to Puerto Vallarta (PVR) and take a taxi to Sayulita. Taxis are $850 pesos with Trip Vallarta. We recommend sharing taxis. You can use this page to organize sharing a taxi: GROUP 🚕 ORGANIZER


You can also take a bus to Sayulita for $40 pesos. The bus stop is across the street from the airport. We marked the exact location of the bus stop on a map below (zoom out from Sayulita to see PVR airport).
